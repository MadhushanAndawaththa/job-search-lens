🔍

Keyword Highlights

Save terms that matter to you. The extension marks them inline on any page — LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, or any company career site you open.

🌫

LinkedIn State Dimming

On LinkedIn Jobs, Viewed, Saved, and Applied cards are quietly dimmed so you can focus on what you haven't processed yet.

📊

Company Stats

Inline company size and LinkedIn follower count appear next to job titles on LinkedIn so you can judge company fit without opening a new tab.

🔒

Fully Local

Keywords and settings stay in chrome.storage.local. Nothing leaves your browser. No logins, no sync, no backend.

How it works

Three steps to a cleaner job search

  1. 1
    Install the extension

    Load it from the GitHub source in developer mode, or install from the Chrome Web Store once published.

  2. 2
    Save your keywords

    Right-click any selected text on any page and choose "Add to Highlighter", or type keywords directly in the popup and pick a colour.

  3. 3
    Scan faster everywhere

    Open any job page — your terms are highlighted instantly. On LinkedIn Jobs, previously processed cards are also dimmed and company stats appear inline.
