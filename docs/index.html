Job Hunt Visualizer

A focused Chrome extension that highlights important job-search keywords and passively dims LinkedIn job cards already marked as Viewed, Saved, or Applied. No accounts, no backend, and no telemetry.

Privacy Policy Support

Purpose

Reduce repetitive scanning on LinkedIn Jobs by surfacing the terms you care about and de-emphasizing cards you have already processed.

Privacy

All keywords and settings stay in chrome.storage.local. The extension does not send job data, user data, or browsing history to external services.
