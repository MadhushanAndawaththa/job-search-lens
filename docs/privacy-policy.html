Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

Job Hunt Visualizer is a local-only Chrome extension for LinkedIn Jobs. This policy explains what the extension accesses, how that information is used, and what is not collected or shared.

What the extension does

The extension highlights saved keywords on LinkedIn job pages and passively dims job cards that LinkedIn itself labels as Viewed, Saved, or Applied.

What data is stored

  • Keywords you save.
  • Color choices for those keywords.
  • Extension settings such as pause state, dim-state toggles, and theme preference.

These values are stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device.

What data is not collected

  • No account information is collected.
  • No personal data is transmitted to the developer.
  • No job application data is uploaded to any server.
  • No analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, or third-party ads are used.
  • No remote code is executed.

How LinkedIn page content is used

The extension reads the content of LinkedIn job pages locally inside the browser in order to:

  • find text matches for your saved keywords, and
  • detect LinkedIn's own Viewed, Saved, and Applied labels already rendered on the page.

This processing happens on-device. The extension does not send LinkedIn page content, browsing history, or job metadata to any external endpoint.

Permissions

  • contextMenus: used to add selected LinkedIn text as a keyword.
  • storage: used to persist your keywords and settings locally.
  • tabs: used so the popup can talk to the active LinkedIn tab.
  • https://www.linkedin.com/*: used to run the content script only on LinkedIn pages.

Data sharing

No stored or processed data is sold, rented, transferred, or disclosed to third parties.
