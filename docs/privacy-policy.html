Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Job Hunt Visualizer is a local-only Chrome extension for LinkedIn Jobs. This policy explains what the extension accesses, how that information is used, and what is not collected or shared.

What the extension does

The extension highlights saved keywords on LinkedIn job pages and passively dims job cards that LinkedIn itself labels as Viewed, Saved, or Applied.

What data is stored

  • Keywords you save.
  • Color choices for those keywords.
  • Extension settings such as pause state, dim-state toggles, and theme preference.

These values are stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

What data is not collected

  • No account information is collected.
  • No personal data is transmitted to the developer or any third party.
  • No job application data is uploaded to any server.
  • No analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising is used.
  • No remote code is executed.

How LinkedIn page content is used

The extension reads LinkedIn job page content locally inside the browser to:

  • find text matches for your saved keywords, and
  • detect LinkedIn’s own Viewed, Saved, and Applied labels already rendered on the page.

This processing is on-device only. The extension does not send LinkedIn page content, browsing history, or job metadata to any external endpoint.

Permissions

  • contextMenus — add selected LinkedIn text as a keyword via right-click.
  • storage — persist your keywords and settings locally in the browser.
  • tabs — let the popup communicate with the active LinkedIn tab.
  • https://www.linkedin.com/* — restrict the content script to LinkedIn pages only.

Data sharing

No stored or processed data is sold, rented, transferred, or disclosed to any third party.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, visit the support page or open an issue on the GitHub issue tracker.
